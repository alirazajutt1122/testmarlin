Portfolio Management
Marlin® has asset class wise portfolio management. Investor can visualize his/her complete portfolio including holding positions among configured asset classes like Equities, Bonds, ETFs etc.: Total investment Current market value of all asset classes Current market value of Equities, Bonds, ETFs separately Accumulative profit/loss Asset class wise profit/loss Total volume traded in Equities, Bonds, ETFs separately
Interactive Graph
Live & historical graph of desired security Investor can view historical graph as per desired time frequency Historical data provides up to 5 years of daily prices and volumes fro each security Real time security stats
Real Market Information
Marlin® is integrated with exchange and all market feed is being updated on real time basis. Investors can get place orders and do trading on live feed.
Personalized Watch List
Marlin® offers comprehensive Market Watch for quick & easy tracking of asset class wise securities. Investors can maintain a personalized list of favorite securities. A quick watchlist is also available on header which can be accessed just by clicking + icon.
Multi Asset Class
Marlin® offers trading in multiple asset classes like Equities Bonds ETFs User can trade in all types of asset classes configured in an integrated exchange.
Virtual Trading
Marlin® has a virtual trading platform where user can practice trading without having money. This trading simulator will give virtual trading experience on real time public market information. Investors can open a demo trading account for free without being registered with a broker. It is being provided to create awareness among potential investors.
